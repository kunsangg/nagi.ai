The Illusion of Thinking: Understanding the Strengths and Limitations of Reasoning Models via the Lens of Problem Complexity

LLaMA OpenSource FoundationModels LanguageModeling Meta

Abstract

Recent generations of frontier language models have introduced Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) that generate detailed thinking processes before providing answers. While these models demonstrate improved performance on reasoning benchmarks, their capabilities under varying problem complexity remain poorly understood...

Introduction

Large Language Models (LLMs) have recently evolved to include specialized variants explicitly designed for reasoning tasks—Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) such as OpenAI's o1/o3, DeepSeek-R1, and Claude 3.7 Sonnet.
